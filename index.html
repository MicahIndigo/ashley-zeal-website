Biography


If the UK R&B/Soul scene is still yet to fully, blossom, then the Birmingham singer-songwriter 'Ashley Zeal' is a name synonymous with its potential. Having honed his musical craft for several years in his hometown, Ashley's honest vulnerable lyricism, contemporary sounds and unequalled vocal tones have garnered him support from some of the most influential names in UK music. His talent is undeniable, his stories relatable and his rise to the summit of the scene, imminent.


Over the years the Birmingham representative has developed his songwriting and performance skills whilst dishing out singles that have been met with increasingly positive responses, reaching thousands of listeners allowing him to build a network and following. This growing success has resulted in both regional and national support including spot plays from BBC WM, BBC 1XTRA and Capital Xtra. With multiple tracks being spun by DJ Target, DJ Ace, Lady Leshurr and Rampage Sound.


2022 marks the year Ashley Zeal released his debut EP 'G.I.R.L'. This projext spotlights his true songwriting abilities and has catapulted this futue R&B star's voice to brand new audiences. As Zeal undertakes the next big steps in his career.


A UK tour covering Leeds, Manchester, Birmingham and London; aswell as performances in treasures such as 'The British Library' and collaborations with Kiss Fresh and Mentos UK in 2024, all set Zeal up for the release of his follow up project 'My Side of the Story'. With this project, the vocalist delives deliberate sonics and lyrics, prpoviding listeners with a truthful insight into his version of events and emotions.
