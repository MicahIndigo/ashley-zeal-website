Biography

If the UK R&B/Soul scene is stiill yet to fully, blossom, then the Birmingham singer-songwriter 'Ashley Zeal' is a name synonymous with its potential. Having honed his mmusical craft for several years in his hometown, Ashley's honest vulnerable lyricism, contemporary sounds and unequalled vocal tones have garnered him support from some of the most influential names in UK music. His talent is undeniable, his stories relatable and his rise to the summit of the scene, imminent.
